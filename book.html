When Effort Becomes The Problem

This book explores the inner struggle of effort, control, healing, awareness, and inner clarity.

At some point, effort itself becomes the problem. The more we try to fix ourselves, the more tension we create. This book explores that inner conflict and the possibility of a different way of living — not through more effort, but through awareness and understanding.
